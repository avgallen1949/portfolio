avrie allen

Avrie is a graphic designer and artist living, working, and (soon-to-be) studying at RISD in Providence. She works with publications & print design, visual identity systems, hand-coded web design, video & motion design, and installation.

Her personal practice centers feminist methodologies/making and explores feminine visual culture, experiences, and feminist consciousness.

She loves the color chartreuse, Born in Flames by Lizzie Borden, and cats.
