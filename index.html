avrie allen

Avrie is a graphic designer and artist living, working, and studying in Bloomington, Indiana. She works with publications & print design, visual identity systems, hand-coded web design, video & animation, and installation.

Her personal practice centers ways women and girls approach making and explores feminine visual culture, experiences, and feminist consciousness.
