Avrie is a mult-disciplinary designer and artist pursuring her MFA in Graphic Design at Rhode Island School of Design. Her personal practice explores feminist methods, the multiplicity and politics of girlhood, and the relationship between design and community-building. She loves working with her hands, combining digital and analog methods in identities, publications, web design and development, and installations.

Avrie has offered free graphic design services (identity, web design, print/web graphics) for community organizations working around issues of incarceration, tenant rights, and workers since 2016.
