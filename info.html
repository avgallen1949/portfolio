Avrie Allen (b. 1997) is living and working in Bloomington, Indiana. She creates content-rich and vibrant publications, videos, posters, digital works, web pages, and physical installations which foreground lively colors, forms, and typography. She allows the form of her works, whether digital, analog, or a combination of both, to be driven by content and research.

Allen believes that art is never neutral. Her practice traverses the politics of feminism, labor, technology, art, and design. At large, she is concerned with feminist consciousness, articulating collective subjectivities, and ways that women and girls approach making.
