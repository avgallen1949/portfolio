Who is Affordable Housing for?

Created for my independent research project during the Fall 2022 semester, I explored the topic of affordable housing initiatives in Bloomington, Indiana. Looking at the proposed solutions to the housing crisis and developments that Bloomington has proposed or put in place over the past decade, the video explores how “affordable housing” development furthers gentrification and displaces working class and low-income people in Bloomington. In addition to furthering gentrification, the cost of these “affordable” units are out of touch with the reality of renters in Bloomington.
