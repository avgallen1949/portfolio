women + technology summit

Visual identity, logo design, print/social media graphics, animations, and swag designs for the Center of Excellence for Women & Technology's annual Women + Technology Summit. The theme softly evokes space and the solar eclipse which will occur during the spring semester that this conference is held. Examples of swag items and large-scale signage below.
