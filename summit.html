Collaborative Women's Summit

Branding, logo design, print and social media graphics for the Center of Excellence for Women & Technology's annual Women + Technology Summit. The theme softly evokes space and the solar eclipse which will occur during the spring semester that this conference is held. Icons reference imagery of space (sun, moon, stars) and the color scheme incorporates both fluorescent, sunny colors and dark gray. Emphasis on contrast was placed on both the color scheme and the choice of typefaces.
